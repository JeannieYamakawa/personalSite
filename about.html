About Jeannie Yamakawa

Hello, world!

I'm Jeannie. I love solving problems, and I've always had an innate need to be creative. Full stack development has provided me the perfect way to utilize all of my logical and creative skills to solve real-world human problems by constructing new technologies that had not previously existed.
