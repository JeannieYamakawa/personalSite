About Jeannie Yamakawa

I love solving problems, and I've always had an innate need to be creative. Software/web development has provided me the perfect way to utilize all of my logical and creative skills to solve real-world human problems by constructing new technologies that had not previously existed.

I found my way to developing by way of my experiences in education and creative media, both of which I continue to pursue in order to help solve human problems.

I have a decade of experience in communications and leadership, having taught and mentored both adults and school-aged children, and also have significant experience in business and project management.

I'm highly-adaptable and thrive in fast-paced, deadline-driven environments, and am excited to be able to apply all of my skills in my career as an engineer.
